EDITORIAL DECISION SUMMARY

Based on three completed reviews, the manuscript has received a composite score of 3.27 out of 5.0. Two reviewers recommend major revision and one recommends acceptance with minor revisions. The primary concerns raised across reviews include insufficient baseline comparisons (specifically the absence of mBART-50 and NLLB-200 benchmarks), limited evaluation on languages beyond the four tested (Turkish, Finnish, Swahili, and Georgian), and questions regarding statistical significance of the reported BLEU score improvements, which range from 1.3 to 4.7 points over baseline depending on language pair.

The editorial board met on May 3, 2024, and voted 3-1 in favor of requesting major revisions rather than rejection. Authors will be given 60 days to submit a revised manuscript.
