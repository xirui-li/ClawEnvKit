SUMMARY OF EDITORIAL DECISION

Based on three completed reviews, the manuscript has been assigned a preliminary decision of Major Revision. The editorial board will communicate this decision to the authors on May 3, 2024, without disclosing reviewer identities.

Key concerns raised across reviews include insufficient baseline comparisons (reviewers 1 and 3 both noted the absence of mBART-50 and NLLB-200 benchmarks), limited evaluation on languages beyond the four tested (Kazakh, Georgian, Amharic, and Basque), and questions about statistical significance reporting. The manuscript's BLEU score improvements of 4.7 to 6.3 points over baseline were noted as promising but require additional validation.

Prof. Lindqvist's more favorable assessment reflects his view that the hierarchical attention architecture represents a genuine methodological contribution, though he also requested clarification on the 12-layer encoder configuration described in Section 3.2.
